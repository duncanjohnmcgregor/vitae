Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the process take?

Most stories are completed within 6-8 weeks from start to finish. The interview phase typically takes 2-3 weeks, followed by 3-4 weeks for writing, editing, and printing.

What if I'm not comfortable with technology?

No tech skills required! Our process is designed to be simple and intuitive. We also provide phone support and can arrange in-person assistance if needed.

How much does it cost?

Our standard package starts at $2,500 and includes the complete process plus one printed copy. Additional copies and premium options are available.

Can I include family photos?

Absolutely! We help you scan and include family photos, documents, and other memorabilia throughout your book. Our design team ensures they're beautifully integrated.

What if I don't remember everything?

That's perfectly normal! Our expert interviewers are trained to help trigger memories and can work with family members to fill in details and verify stories.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. All recordings and documents are encrypted, and we never share your personal information. You retain full ownership of your story.
